Clams Sukha

Welcome to Kitchen Treat, where we invite you to experience the coastal charm with our tantalizing Clam (Tisrya) Sukha. Dive into a world where succulent clams are kissed with aromatic spices, creating a dish that’s as bold as it is flavorful. Our Clam (Tisrya) Sukha is a culinary gem, meticulously crafted to capture the essence of coastal indulgence. Each bite offers a symphony of textures and tastes, with the delicate brininess of the clams perfectly complementing the robust flavours of our masala blend. At Kitchen Treat, we’re committed to bringing the taste of the sea to your doorstep, ensuring every serving of our Clam (Tisrya) Sukha is a memorable culinary escapade. Join us as we embark on a gastronomic journey where every clam tells a story of oceanic bliss and coastal enchantment.

Prawns Curry

Step into a culinary sanctuary where the robust Flavors of coastal cuisine reign supreme. Our prawns Curry pays homage to Goan’s rich culinary heritage, boasting a harmonious blend of spices and fresh prawns cooked to perfection. Each aromatic spoonful encapsulates the essence of Goan’s culinary tradition, delivering a symphony of Flavors that dance on your palate. Surmai Fry

“Surmai fry” is more than just a dish – it’s a journey through taste, tradition, and style. With its succulent Kingfish, rich spices, and meticulous preparation, each bite tells a story of coastal heritage and culinary expertise passed down through generations. It’s a flavourful tribute to the bounty of the sea and the skill of those who transform it into a crispy masterpiece.
