Melamine Fine Dining Products

Kitchentreats deals in 100% pure melamine fine dining products which are associated with Srithai Superware India limited in its brand ektra, for leading kitchenware and tableware. Our Director Siddhi Padgaonkar having two decades of rich experience in sales, out of which, 12 years in the business. She has Bachelors Degree in Psychology and Management & Secretarial Courses to her credit. Her education coupled with sales experience made KitchenTreats a customer centric company where they give maximum benefit to their customers with low profit margin and their costs are very competitive. Clients include reputed leading corporate houses, hotels, restaurants and interior designing firms across the country.

VISION

Reaching to every household to decorate with colourful kitchenware that ensure safety,
durability and hygienic quality standards.

MISSION

Empowering woman strength by creating network of enthusiast, energetic and enterprising
females who dare to change colours of each kitchen.
